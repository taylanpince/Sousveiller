Sousveiller is an application for iOS devices that lets you turn the tables on surveillance in your environment.

Use your phone or iPod to take pictures of instances of surveillance technology you encounter. Spottings are automatically geotagged and visible to other Sousveiller users.

Search for cameras around you using the Sousveiller app and on sousveiller.com
